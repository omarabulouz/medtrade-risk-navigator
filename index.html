GDP - Good Distribution Practice

Quality standards for distribution of pharmaceutical products to ensure integrity and quality throughout the supply chain.

SFDA - Saudi Food and Drug Authority

Saudi regulatory body responsible for approving and monitoring pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and related products.

Cold Chain - Temperature-Controlled Supply Chain

Unbroken series of refrigerated production, storage, and distribution activities maintaining specific temperature ranges (e.g., 2¬∞C-8¬∞C for vaccines).

Voyage - Ocean Transport Journey

Complete sea journey from port of loading to port of discharge, typically 2-4 weeks for shipments from Europe to Saudi Arabia.

ICC - Institute Cargo Clauses

Standard marine insurance terms: ICC A (comprehensive), ICC B (named perils), ICC C (basic coverage - minimum for CIP/CIF).

Halal Certification - Islamic Compliance Certificate

Required documentation proving pharmaceutical ingredients (especially gelatin capsules) comply with Islamic dietary laws.

GMP - Good Manufacturing Practice

Quality assurance standards ensuring pharmaceutical products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards.

Insurance Liability Gap - Risk vs Cost Responsibility Mismatch

Critical situation where seller pays transport costs but buyer bears loss risk (CPT/CFR) or where minimum insurance may be inadequate for pharmaceutical cargo value.

ICC A Coverage - Institute Cargo Clauses A - Comprehensive

Highest level marine insurance covering all risks except specific exclusions. Required for CIP under Incoterms 2020, suitable for high-value pharmaceuticals.
